Cost & Insurance
How Much Does a Visit Cost?
Fortunately for you, the cost for Mobile One Docs coming to your home to take care of you is about the same as when you take
the effort and time to go to an office-based doctor visit. Regarding billing and insurance, we are required to follow your health
insurance’s rules regarding co-pays and deductibles the same way that everybody else (other medical practices) are.



What if I don't have Insurance?
Mobile One Docs charges a flat fee of $150 at the time of visit via credit card.
We accept most insurance plans. To prevent confusion, our intake staff checks your specific plan benefits prior to scheduling you.
